How the score is calculated
Interest Rate (25%)

Higher rates → stronger currency. Hawkish surprises weighted positively.

CPI / Inflation (20%)

Above-target inflation that prompts rate hikes scores bullish; runaway inflation scores bearish.

GDP Growth (20%)

Positive growth versus expectations is bullish for the currency and risk assets.

Employment (20%)

Low unemployment & strong NFP/jobs beats drive currency strength.

PMI / Trade (15%)

PMI above 50 signals expansion. Trade surplus nations receive a positive premium.
